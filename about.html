Log #1

2/12/20
The client assigned us to create a food ordering app to compete with current mainstream food ordering services. Some of the features they want are the ability to set food preferences and create profiles (like if they like Italian food or desserts). There should also be search options for food preferences and blacklisted options (you don’t want vegan food to show up). The name and aesthetics of the website is up to us.
